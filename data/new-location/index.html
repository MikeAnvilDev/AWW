New Sampling Site From

Sampling Sites: Remember the general factors to consider when selecting a water monitoring site: to be safe, convenient and accessible, to have legal access and to be strategic. Optimal water monitoring sites are those that provide the best information to satisfy objectives with the least amount of effort. Choose a site that is not too difficult or dangerous to access and is strategically located to be tested in an efficient manner. It is essential to know the precise location of a monitoring site for full use of the data. Please carefully describe your site information, and submit this form with your first set of data taken at the site.
