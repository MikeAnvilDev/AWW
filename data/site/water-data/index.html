Water Data

Home » Water Data

Alabama Water Watch has developed a suite of online tools for storing, retrieving, examining and exploring AWW water data. Citizen volunteer monitors, and the public, can access AWW water chemistry and bacteriological monitoring data, displayed as line graphs and bar graphs by clicking one of the two links below.

Bacteriological Data ( bar graphs of E. coli per 100 milliliters of water) and Water Chemistry Data (line graphs of air and water temperature, dissolved oxygen, pH, alkalinity, hardness, turbidity, salinity, and Secchi disk visibility) graphs can be generated by clicking on either the Data by Table or on the Data by Map below. Click here for more detailed instructions on generating graphs and exploring AWW water data.
